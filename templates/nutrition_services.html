A well balanced diet, along with regular exercise, is an essential part of a healthy lifestyle. A Registered Dietician can help you maximize your results by giving you all of the information you need to make healthy choices a part of your every day life. Make the most of your workouts and treat your body right with Sweat It Out Fitness and Inspired Nutrition.

Kristy Leavitt, R.D.

Kristy Leavitt is a Registered Dietitian, registered with the College of Dietitians of Manitoba. Kristy completed her Bachelor of Science in Human Nutrition Sciences at the University of Manitoba and a dietetic internship with Manitoba Partnership Dietetic Education Program in Winnipeg, MB.

In addition to Inspired Nutrition, Kristy has experience working in the areas of chronic disease prevention and management (primarily type 2 diabetes and cardiovascular disease), infant and child nutrition, and nutrition during pregnancy.

An avid baker and cook, Kristy loves creating healthy, easy meals without sacrificing great taste.

Kristy lives with her amazing husband Stephen in Winnipeg.

To schedule a consultation with Kristy visit her at Inspired Nutrition.
