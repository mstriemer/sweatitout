Jenna

Jenna Hobson is a Certified Personal Trainer with a Physical Education degree from the University of Manitoba. As a member of the Manitoba Fitness Council Jenna holds certifications in Group Fitness, Resistance Training and Yoga. She currently attends the University of Manitoba Education program with the goal of becoming a Junior High Phys Ed teacher. Jenna is a long time member of the Rowdies rugby team and along with daily workouts she also plays recreational volleyball. Her passion for fitness is a huge part of her daily life and she brings this passion and expertise to her group fitness classes and personal training sessions.
To book a free personal training consultation with Jenna Hobson email jenna@sweatitoutfit.com.
